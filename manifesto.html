The Manifesto

The FLOQ Manifesto

We are uncovering better ways of developing software in the AI era. Through this work we have come to value the left over the right — while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.

🎯
Outcomes over Output
We value this more than ↓
Shipped features that move business metrics over completed story points
🌊
Flow over Ceremony
We value this more than ↓
Continuous delivery of value over scheduled rituals
📡
Signals over Schedules
We value this more than ↓
Responding to reality over following a calendar
🤖
AI Partnership over Manual Labor
We value this more than ↓
Augmenting human creativity with AI capability over humans writing every line
🚀
Speed with Quality over Planned Perfection
We value this more than ↓
Shipping fast with automated quality gates over meticulous sprint plans
